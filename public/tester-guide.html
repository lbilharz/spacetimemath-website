Official Guide (12-Person Group)

1. The Teacher Role (1 Person)
  1. Sign Up & Elevate Open the app, set a username, and go to the Account tab. Click "Teacher" and enter your email address to validate. The secure 6-digit code will permanently unlock your Teacher tools.
  2. Create a Classroom Navigate to the Classes tab and tap "Create Class". Name it (e.g., Alpha Testers).
  3. Invite the Group The app generates a 6-character Join Code. You can print the login cards or simply display the live QR code on a screen for the 11 students to scan.
2. The Student Role (11 Persons)
  1. Join the Class Open the app (no account/email required). Pick your username. Go to Classes → Join Class and enter the 6-character code (or scan the Teacher's QR code).
  2. Connect as Friends Go to the Friends tab. Tap "Create Invite Link" and share your code with a peer. You can now compete on your private leaderboard.
  3. Setup Push Reminders Go to Account → App Settings → Push Reminders. Set up a daily reminder to build your training habit!
3. The Live Class Test (Together)
  1. Launch the Diagnostic Sprint Once all 11 students are actively visible as "Online" in the Classroom view, the Teacher taps "Start Sprint" and selects the Diagnostic Sprint.
  2. Answer the Problems All 11 student devices instantly shift into the 60-second sprint. Answer as accurately and quickly as possible!
  3. Review the Mastery Grid When the countdown ends, everyone can review their results. The Teacher will see the collective 10x10 *Classroom Mastery Grid* uncovering exactly where the group struggled most.
4. Individual Progression
  1. Personal Analytics Students can open their Progress tab. Review the personal Mastery Grid to find the weakest concepts and strongest skills mathematically mapped by the engine.
  2. Train Independently Start a Training Sprint directly from the Lobby. The adaptive difficulty engine will now safely challenge you in your optimal learning zone.
  3. Climb the Ranks Check the Friends or Classroom tabs to see how your weighted points compare on the leaderboards.
  4. Unlock the 20x20 Domain Continue answering core problems correctly to push your 80% Mastery threshold. Hitting targets will unlock higher tiers, expanding your capabilities up to the absolute 20x20 bounds!
Pro Tip: Please remind the 11 student testers to save their permanent Recovery Keys (found under Settings) if they want to retain their points and data across future app version updates.
